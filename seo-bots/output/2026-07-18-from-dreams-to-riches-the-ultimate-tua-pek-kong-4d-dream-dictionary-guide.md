## The Sacred Art of Tua Pek Kong Dream Decoding

The Tua Pek Kong Dream Dictionary is a cultural treasure. Named after the beloved Taoist God of Prosperity, this system assigns specific 2D, 3D, and 4D numbers to everyday objects, animals, and scenarios encountered in dreams. The belief is simple: when your subconscious mind is active, spiritual guides present symbols to help you navigate your earthly fortune.

## 3 Common Dreams and Their Secret 4D Numbers

### 1. Dreaming of Snakes (The Ultimate Wealth Omen)
In Chinese culture, the snake is often referred to as a "little dragon." Dreaming of a snake is one of the most powerful signs of incoming wealth. 
* **The Interpretation:** If you dream of a snake slithering quietly, it represents hidden fortune. If the snake bites you, do not panic! A snake bite in a dream means a sudden windfall is heading your way.
* **The Lucky Numbers:** According to the dictionary, a snake corresponds to the number **026** or **726**. For a full 4D combination, many players go with **5267** or **1026**.

### 2. Dreaming of Fish (Abundance and Prosperity)
The Chinese word for fish, "Yu" (鱼), sounds exactly like the word for surplus or abundance (余). 
* **The Interpretation:** To dream of swimming fish means your cash flow will be smooth and unrestricted. If you dream of catching a fish with your bare hands, it means you are about to "catch" a major lottery prize.
* **The Lucky Numbers:** Fish are associated with the numbers **03**, **143**, and **8043**. If the fish is golden, try adding the number 8, resulting in combinations like **8143**.

### 3. Dreaming of Money (Financial Windfalls)
It might seem too literal, but dreaming of finding money is a highly auspicious sign.
* **The Interpretation:** Finding paper cash on the floor means unexpected opportunities are coming. However, if you dream of losing money, it is actually a reverse omen—meaning a payout is imminent.
* **The Lucky Numbers:** Paper money translates to **522** or **988**. If you dream of gold coins, the classic lucky combination is **8118** or **1188**.
